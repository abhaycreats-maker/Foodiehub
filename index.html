Our Story

Born from Passion,
Crafted with Soul

Zafran was born in the heart of Mumbai in 2009, when Chef Rahul Anand returned from a decade of culinary training across Paris, Tokyo, and New York with a singular vision — to reimagine Indian cuisine through the lens of global fine dining.

Every dish at Zafran is a conversation between heritage and innovation. We source directly from small farmers across India, honour ancient recipes, and elevate them with modern technique. The result is something timeless — food that tells a story with every bite.

Our dining room is a sanctuary. Designed by award-winning firm Studio Noir, every element — from the handwoven silk panels to the amber candlelight — is crafted to cocoon you in warmth and wonder.
